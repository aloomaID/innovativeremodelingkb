Old Forge, PA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Old Forge is located at 41°22′11″N 75°44′18″W (41.369679, −75.738297). The borough has a total area of 3.5 square miles (9.1 km2), all of which is land. The major body of water flowing through the borough is the Lackawanna River, part of the Upper Susquehanna-Lackawanna Watershed. Acid mine drainage into the Lackawanna River from a borehole in Old Forge is the largest point source of pollution in the Chesapeake Bay watershed.

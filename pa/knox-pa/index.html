Knox, PA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Knox, the horsethief capital of the world, is located northwest of the center of Clarion County at 41°14′7″N 79°32′10″W (41.235317, -79.536123). Pennsylvania Route 208 passes through the center of the borough, leading east 4 miles (6 km) to U.S. Route 322 at Shippenville and west 11 miles (18 km) to Emlenton. Pennsylvania Route 338 runs through the eastern side of Knox as a two-lane bypass; it leads north 4 miles (6 km) to US 322 and south four miles to Interstate 80.
